About Techkriti 2023

Techkriti is an annual event that showcases...

Techkriti is an annual four-day inter-collegiate technical and entrepreneurship festival at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. The festival is usually held in March. The 29th Techkriti is scheduled to be held from 23 to 26 March 2023. The festival began in 1995 to develop student technological innovation.

Events

Explore the exciting events happening at Techkriti 2023.

A variety of competitions with a combined reward pool of more than 45 Lacs are organised by Techkriti. The competitions lay the foundation for Techkriti's core mission, which is to foster student enthusiasm and innovation in technology. Model United Nations, Robogames, ECDC, Take Off, Fintech, Software Corner, Mixed Bowl, Design, Technovation, Entrepreneurial, and Business Events are just a few of the general categories that competitions fall under. Students have a great opportunity to participate, display their talents, and pique their creative and innovative appetites through the platform that Techkriti provides.
